About ByteDefenders

We are a synergy of students at the University of Makeni (UNIMAK), that is aimed at raising awareness on personal security, phishing targets, to individuals and organizations and deepen their understanding of legal implications in cyberspace. This awareness program will provide a holistic approach to cybersecurity through education, and resources designed to build a resilient digital environment.
